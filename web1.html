IPL Details

The Indian Premier League (IPL), also known as TATA IPL for sponsorship reasons,
is a men's T20 franchise cricket league of India. It is annually contested by ten teams based out of seven Indian cities and three Indian states.
The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007.
